Frequently Asked Questions

❓ What is credit repair?

Credit repair refers to the process of identifying and disputing errors on your credit report, aiming to improve your credit score.

❓ Can I repair my own credit?

Yes, you can attempt to repair your credit yourself. However, professional services often provide more accurate results due to their expertise and access to advanced tools.
